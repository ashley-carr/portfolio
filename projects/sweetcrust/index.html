SweetCrust Bakery Portal

A comprehensive customer portal enabling online orders, delivery tracking, and relationship management.

Overview

SweetCrust Bakery needed a modern digital platform to streamline customer interactions and manage orders efficiently. We designed and built a comprehensive customer portal using Power Apps Canvas and Dataverse as the data backbone.

The portal enables customers to browse products, place orders, track deliveries in real-time, and manage their account preferences. Behind the scenes, Power Automate workflows automate the order processing pipeline and send notifications to the bakery team.

Challenge

SweetCrust Bakery was managing orders manually through email and phone calls, leading to:

  • • High order processing times and errors
  • • No visibility into delivery status for customers
  • • Difficulty tracking customer preferences and repeat orders
  • • Manual data entry across multiple systems

Solution

We built an end-to-end digital ordering and fulfillment system:

  • Customer Portal: Canvas app allowing customers to browse products, place orders, and track deliveries
  • Data Management: Dataverse tables modeling customers, products, orders, and deliveries
  • Order Processing: Power Automate workflows that validate orders, route to bakery, and send notifications
  • Real-time Updates: Customers receive SMS/email notifications at each order stage
  • Analytics: Dashboard to track order metrics and customer trends
