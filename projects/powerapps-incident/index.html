Power Apps Incident Management

Enterprise incident management system with real-time dashboards and automated escalations.

Model-Driven Apps Dataverse Power Automate Power BI

Overview

A mining company required a comprehensive incident management system to track, escalate, and resolve safety and operational incidents across multiple sites. We designed and built a Model-Driven Power App on Dataverse with integrated Power Automate workflows and Power BI analytics.

The system enables rapid incident reporting, automatic escalation based on severity, team collaboration, and compliance reporting—all with real-time visibility for executives.

Challenge

The client struggled with:

  • • Fragmented incident reporting across email, spreadsheets, and manual logs
  • • Delayed escalation causing safety risks
  • • No audit trail or compliance reporting
  • • Difficulty identifying trends and recurring issues
  • • Poor cross-site collaboration and communication

Solution

We built an integrated incident management platform:

  • Incident Capture: Mobile-friendly forms for field workers to report incidents in real-time
  • Automated Escalation: Power Automate workflows escalate high-severity incidents to management
  • Collaboration: Teams can view incidents, add notes, assign actions, and track resolution
  • Compliance Reporting: Power BI dashboards track metrics and generate compliance reports
  • Audit Trail: Complete history of all incident changes and approvals
  • Mobile Support: Responsive design works on phones, tablets, and desktops

Key Features

Incident Creation
